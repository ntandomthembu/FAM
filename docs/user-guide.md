# User Guide for FMD Control Platform

## Introduction
The FMD Control Platform is designed to facilitate the early detection, containment, and management of Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD) outbreaks in livestock. This user guide provides an overview of the platform's features and how to effectively use them.

## Getting Started
To use the FMD Control Platform, you need to register and create an account. Once registered, you can access various features tailored to your role, whether you are a farmer, veterinarian, or government authority.

## Features

### 1. Reporting Sick Animals
Farmers can easily report suspected cases of FMD through the mobile app:
- Open the app and tap on "Report Sick Animals."
- Fill out the short form with details such as farm location, number of affected animals, species, symptoms, and upload photos.
- The GPS location is automatically captured, and the report is submitted to the authorities.

### 2. Outbreak Intelligence Map
The platform features a live outbreak map that displays:
- Suspected and confirmed outbreaks
- Containment zones and risk zones
Authorities can monitor patterns and receive alerts for clusters of reports.

### 3. Veterinary Investigation
Once a report is submitted, it is assigned to a local veterinarian who will:
- Investigate the farm
- Update the case status in the system (e.g., under investigation, confirmed outbreak, resolved)

### 4. Livestock Traceability
In the event of an outbreak, the platform tracks:
- Livestock purchases and movements
- Auction participation and transportation routes
This allows for effective tracing of the disease spread.

### 5. Quarantine and Movement Control
The platform manages:
- Quarantine zones and livestock movement permits
- Farmers can apply for movement permits digitally, which authorities can approve or deny based on outbreak risk.

### 6. Vaccination Campaign Management
During outbreaks, the platform tracks:
- Vaccine stock and vaccination campaigns
- Farmers can request vaccinations directly, and regional vets will schedule visits.

### 7. Early Warning Alerts
Farmers receive alerts via:
- Mobile push notifications, SMS, or WhatsApp
These alerts inform them of nearby outbreaks or movement bans.

### 8. Farm Biosecurity Guidance
The app provides resources for farmers, including:
- Symptom guides, biosecurity checklists, and sanitation protocols to help prevent disease spread.

### 9. AI Disease Detection (Future Feature)
Farmers will be able to upload photos of animals, and AI will analyze them for signs of FMD, providing early detection capabilities.

### 10. National Disease Dashboard
Authorities have access to a central dashboard displaying:
- Outbreak heatmaps, active cases, response times, and vaccination coverage.

### 11. Export and Trade Compliance
The platform generates necessary documents for maintaining international trade credibility, including:
- Disease-free zone certificates and livestock health reports.

## Conclusion
The FMD Control Platform is a comprehensive tool designed to enhance the management of Foot-and-Mouth Disease outbreaks. By utilizing its features, users can contribute to the early detection and effective response to FMD, ultimately protecting livestock and the agricultural economy. For further assistance, please refer to the support section within the app or contact your local veterinary authority.
